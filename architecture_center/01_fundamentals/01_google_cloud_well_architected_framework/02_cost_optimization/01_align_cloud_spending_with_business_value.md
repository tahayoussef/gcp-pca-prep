# Align cloud spending with business value

- **Prioritize Managed Services and Serverless**
    - Use services like **Cloud SQL**, **GKE Autopilot**, and **Dataproc Serverless** to reduce operational overhead.
    - Lowers Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) compared to managing VMs or on-premises infrastructure.

- **Balance Cost Efficiency with Business Agility**
    - flexible infrastructure enables rapid innovation.
    - Adopt **DORA metrics** (deployment frequency, lead time) to optimize delivery.
    - Follow **SRE practices** (automation, observability) to improve reliability and prevent costly outages.

- **Enable Self-Service Optimization**
    - Empower teams with tools to provision and manage resources autonomously.
    - Fosters ownership and accountability.

- **Adopt FinOps**
    - Establish a collaborative culture where teams make informed decisions balancing cost and value.
    - Promotes financial accountability.

- **Promote a Value-Driven Mindset**
    - Focus on **TCO**, not just upfront costs.
    - Use **Value Stream Mapping** to analyze value flow.
    - Implement **Unit Costing** to understand cost drivers per application/service.
