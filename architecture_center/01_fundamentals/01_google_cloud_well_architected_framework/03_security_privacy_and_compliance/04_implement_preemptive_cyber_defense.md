# Implement preemptive cyber defense

- **Integrate Cyber Defense Functions**
    - **Intelligence**: Threat profile, TTPs (Google SecOps, Mandiant).
    - **Detect & Respond**: Core active defense (logging, triage).
    - **Validate**: Continuous assurance of controls.
    - **Hunt**: Proactive search for threats.
    - **Mission Control**: Strategy and coordination.

- **Use Intelligence**
    - **Google SecOps**: Centralized security operations, threat hunting, curated detections.
    - **Mandiant**: Frontline threat intelligence.
    - **VirusTotal**: Enrichment of threat data.

- **Capitalize on Defender's Advantage**
    - Use knowledge of your own environment to effectively monitor and defend.
    - **Chrome Enterprise Premium**: Browser-based threat/data protection.
    - **Security Command Center Enterprise**: Risk engine for prioritization.

- **Validate and Improve Defenses**
    - Continuous validation via **Penetration Testing**, **Red/Blue/Purple Teaming**, and **Tabletop Exercises**.
    - Use **Cloud NGFW** for network threat prevention.
